Portfolio

Project 1 -mkulima.ai

"Mkulima.ai" is a digital platform designed to provide localized agricultural information to farmers. Through this project, farmers can access a wealth of knowledge regarding farming methods, suitable inputs, insecticides, and prevailing farming conditions tailored to their specific region or locality. The platform employs advanced AI algorithms to analyze and interpret data, offering personalized recommendations and insights to optimize farming practices and enhance crop yields. By leveraging technology, Mkulima.ai aims to empower farmers with the tools and information they need to make informed decisions, increase productivity, and improve overall agricultural outcomes in their communities.

Project 2-Construction Guru app

"Construction Guru" is a specialized online platform designed to streamline the process of hiring skilled casual construction workers for specific construction projects. This website serves as a convenient marketplace where contractors and builders can connect with experienced laborers on an as-needed basis. At the core of "Construction Guru" is a comprehensive database of skilled casual construction workers, ranging from general laborers to specialized tradespeople such as carpenters, electricians, plumbers, and masons. Contractors can browse through profiles, filtering workers based on their skills, experience, location, and availability. The website features a user-friendly interface that allows contractors to post job listings detailing the specific requirements of their construction projects, including the type of work, duration, and any specialized skills needed. Skilled workers can then view these listings and apply for jobs that match their expertise and schedule.
